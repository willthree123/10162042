Industry IoT

Factory IoT

In 2019, IDC estimates the manufacturing segment invested close to $200 billion in IoT spending, twice as much as the consumer IoT segment, the second largest IoT vertical market. The term “industrial Internet of Things” has a more muted-sounding promise of driving operational efficiencies through automation, connectivity and analytics.There are four main functions of IoT that can be used in manufacturing industries:

  1. predictive maintenance
  2. remoting production control
  3. asset tracking
  4. logistics management

Predictive maintenance

Like intel, use networked Industrial IoT sensors on the assembly line, smart manufacturing tracks key indicators of equipment wear — like vibration and temperature .

Mi home

Chinese technology company XiaoMi invented their brand’s IoT smart home which is called Mi Home. It is one of the biggest companies that sells smart home products. And it also teamed up with some big western companies for example Philip and Honeywell. And it has sold over 10 million of Smart Home products from the start of this project to 2017.

It has some products like smart band, door lock, combo washer dryer, kitchen exhaust hood, stove top set, induction cooker, AI translator, rollerball pen, and hover shoes and a lot more. It has a Mi Home App which can communicate with smart home furniture and devices so that users can manage devices, view telemetries, set up alerts and notification only by using their smartphone. It also can maximize home security by using their Smart Security products like monitors and sensors. It also offers energy savings because you can have a more precise control and the gadgets are often utilized less energy than other normal gadgets.

Another example is the Mi Smart LED Smart Bulb, which also is a part of the Mi Home product, it is not like a normal LED bulb it offers about 16 million colors for customers to change. However, how to change the color? It offers two ways to control the light bulb including the lightness and changing color. As it is a smart home device so the first way is that customers can use their phone to control the LED smart bulb. It also supports voice control, activating dazzling lighting with just a few words.
